About

Speaking is such an important skill for our everyday lives, yet I never really try to dive deep in this area. JDReads is gonna be a podcast style thing. I will try to find an article that interests me and read it aloud for improvements. The goal is set for one article a day, but life gets busy sometimes. Anyways, Hopefully by the end of the series, I will have enough confidence speaking to anyone anywhere.
