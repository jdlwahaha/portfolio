People

These are the list of people that changed my life. I don't agree 100% to whatever they say, but some of the concepts that they shared helped shaped the person who I am today.

Project Life Mastery - Stefan James

His video about how to make more money opens my eyes for me. He's the first YouTuber that introduced me to the world of self-development.

Andrew Tate

Despite his controversy, I think he does make some strong arguments about how to be stronger and live life to the fullest. To be the best verison of yourself.

Dan Lok

His "Boss in the Bently" series did help me grow as a person. As an immigrant and English not as first language, I used to look up to him until he introduced HTC and his second book.

陳之漢 - 館長
