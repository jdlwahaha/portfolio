Card Storage

I came across Gamegenic card boxes Check it out on Amazon.ca when I was searching for better boxes for these kitten cards. I wanted more portable and accessible card storage than the default packages. Some of the boxes (especially the expansion packs) are way too oversized and takes too much effort to open.

I would say that Gamegenic makes pretty premium card boxes! They use strong magnets for the lid and the cases are mostly made out of fabrics. These boxes also come in different sizes and colours that you will for sure find one that tailors your need.

I would of course have the yellow one. Just looks nice :D

Card Sleeves

Gamegenic also makes card sleeves with variety of colour selections as well if you want protect the cards from playing. Sleeves are also useful if one of your cards has damaged edges or back. It can help hiding damaged marks from identifying incoming cards (or kittens).
