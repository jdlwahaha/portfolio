Card Storage

While I think Exploding Kittens is a fun party game and all, they could have made the boxes more portable and usable. Most of the boxes (especially the expansion ones) are way too oversized and take too much effort to open. Party Pack is the only one that I found the case being convenient at all. The rest of them are pretty much for shelf. I don't want to leave the rest of the cards in there. I want to make it more accessible to take it everywhere and play with others.

Gamegenic

I came across Gamegenic Check it out on Amazon.ca during my research on this topic. I would say that they do make pretty premium card storage! They use strong magnets for the lids and the case mostly made out of fabrics. They make these boxes come in different sizes and colours that you will for sure find one that tailors your need.

If you need some sleeves to protect the cards, they also make those and with variety of colour selections as well. So if say one of your card has an damaged edges or back and friends can see an exploding kittens coming, putting the cards in sleeves will help hiding those marks.

Anyways, I think it's a pretty nice brand that worths sharing. Check out more Gamegenic products on Amazon and have fun shopping!
