Gamegenic

I came across Gamegenic card boxes Check it out on Amazon.ca when I was searching for better boxes for these kitten cards. I wanted more portable and accessible card storage than the default packages. Some of the boxes (especially the expansion packs) are way too oversized and takes too much effort to open.

I would say that Gamegenic makes pretty premium card boxes! They use strong magnets for the lid and the cases are mostly made out of fabrics. These boxes also come in different sizes and colours that you will for sure find one that tailors your need.

I would of course have the yellow one. Just looks nice :D

Oomomo

Gamegenic makes really premium card boxes, but if you are looking for a cheaper alternative, I would like to recommend these plastic boxes from Oomomo. It is very light-weight and comes with a lock and a handle for easy-carrying. It is also stackable and space-saving.

The only drawback is that there's a inner bump for the lock mechanism that can potentially damage the cards when you close the case. Therefore, I have the field guide and instructions paper placed at the front to protect the cards.

They only have black, gray, and white handles. No yellow :(

Exploding Kittens

I really wish Exploding Kittens could make better boxes. Party Pack was the only perfect box in turns of the size and usability. It is easy to flip open and is a fun party singing box! However, the rest of the boxes are mostly oversized and not convenient to open. I hope they can make more boxes similar to the Party Pack version so that we don't have to spend extra for the card boxes in the future.
