The following books have almost the same content as Cigars: The Ultimate Guide to Cigar Smoking by Clarice Stevenson



Cigars

by Tiziana M.

This one feels like a straight out copy of the Cigar book by Clarice Stevenson. It was slightly restructured and paraphrased, but the ideas are basically the same.



Cigars: The Complete Guide from Novice to Expert

written by Renee Perez, narrated by Matt Montanez

This is an audiobook. The content is slightly restructured and paraphrased from the book by Clarice Stevenson, but the narrator did a great job reading out this book.
